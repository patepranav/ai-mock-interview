# Ai Interview Mocker

Welcome to our innovative platform, a unique website that generates mock interview questions using the advanced Gemini API. Our goal is to help individuals prepare for their upcoming interviews by providing them with realistic and tailored practice questions. Navigate through our site and take a step forward in your interview preparation journey today!

## Key Features:-
# User-Friendly Interface: Easy sign-in or sign-up on a sleek landing page.
# Dashboard: Effortlessly manage your mock interviews.
# Custom Interview Setup: Add new interviews with job details.
# AI-Generated Questions: Receive 5 tailored questions based on your inputs.
# Interactive Interview Process: View details, start interviews, and choose to read or listen to questions.
# Record Your Answers: Easily record and submit your responses.
# Comprehensive Feedback: Get detailed feedback and ratings from #GenerativeAI.
# Review Past Interviews: Access and track your previous interviews.
